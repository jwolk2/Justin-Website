Depth Journey

In this game, users explore the connection between ocean depth and musical register to create their own musical composition. Users visit three ocean zones and select species commonly found in each. They then select a musical instrument that plays in the same low, middle, or upper register in order to match the low, middle, or upper depths in that ocean zone. In the final screen, all species and instruments perform their melodies together in one, combined musical composition.
