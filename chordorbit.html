Chord Orbit Composer

To explore the connection between gravity in planetary orbits and gravity in musical harmony, users can drag and drop chords to create a repeating chord cycle. In choosing between three planets, the chord cycle length is representative of that planet's orbital rate. The users are encouraged to select chords that share the same degree of gravitational force as that planet experiences in relation to the sun. Once the user creates the chord cycle, they can press play to hear the music while watching the planet orbit around the sun.
