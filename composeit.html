Compose-It!

Using this graphic notation program, users of all ages can compose without any previous musical knowledge or training. The shapes at the bottom of the screen are representative of notes of varying lengths and durations. The user drags these rhythmic shapes onto the graph to compose a melody. Users have the choice to compose melodies in triple or duple meter. The playback feature allows users to hear their melodies performed and a download button allows them to save their work to their computer.
